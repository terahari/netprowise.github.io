Services

Our list of awesome services.

We teach just about any course you can devise in the domain of Computer Networking & Security. The content is incredibly flexible, and is always specialized to meet the needs of each and every client. You will be liaising directly with one of us, to finalize the course content & schedule that works best for you. Here, we've made a few suggestions based on our past experiences & courses taught. Most topics involve a fair amount of hands-on learning. The final course content can include any/all of these, a unique combination, or even other topics that suit your needs!

TCP/IP Networking

Rich in hands-on, covers basic and advanced topics like IPv6, IoT. Protocol development, Socket programming, monitoring and troubleshooting of networking problems

NMS and OSS

Includes SNMP based NMS development - MIB and management applications design and development. Web Technologies in NMS, TNM, CMIP, NGOSS guidelines - eTOM and lower layers. Java support for NGOSS – Selective JSR. Issues in controlling and managing smart devices.

Multimedia and IMS

Multimedia representation and transport, IPTV. IMS and IMS with LTE – VoLTE, Message level (Wireshark) coverage of end-to-end procedures, LTE core network, IMS – Convergence and Interworking, IP mobile solution - PMIP. Tunneling in LTE with Wireshark trace.

VoIP and SIP

Challenges in implementing VoIP. Complete hands-on with live SIP network. MGCP and H.323. Related topics – DNS, NAPTR, SDP, Multimedia Servers, IMDS, Diameter and RADIUS.

Information Security

Intrusion and Intrusion Detection, Encryption, Authentication, Tunneling. Sniffing and Spoofing with Winpcap, AAA – Diameter and RADIUS. Information Security foundation – Number Theory, Elliptical Curve Cryptography.

Network Programming & Tools

Socket Programming, Developing Protocols. Developing Networking monitoring, IDS tools with Link layer API and SNMP. Wireshark and Cisco’s PacketTracer, and some home-grown tools.

About Us

This is what we are.

Netprowise was founded in 2004 by Dr. Hari. The mission was to bridge the gap that he had seen in his long career as an Engineer - the gap between the academic knowledge that students graduate with, and the hands-on skills that a true Engineer must possess. Dr. Hari received his Electrical Engineering degree with a scholarship leading to distinction from IISc, Bangalore, and his doctoral degree in Computer Science with a fellowship from Concordia University, Montreal. After his PhD, he worked with Bell Northern Research, followed by Nortel Networks, TeraOptics Networks (a Silicon Valley start-up) and SRA Systems (Chennai). This move to Chennai is what prompted him to give back to the education system that had taken him to some of the leading institutions and organizations abroad. He has developed a number of proprietary networking training programs. He has trained educators on his innovative networking training methodology “Live Lab in your Backpack”. We have successfully completed hundreds of high-quality, hands-on courses since it's modest beginnings is 2004. In the past few years, we've been able to expand our reach outside of India as well - to China, Taiwan, SA, Kenya, Georgia, and Mexico.
